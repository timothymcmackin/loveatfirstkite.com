Travel info

Airports

RDU airport (Raleigh-Durham)
Nearest airport, about 20 minutes from downtown Durham.
CLT airport (Charlotte, NC)
The Charlotte airport is about 2 hours and 45 minutes from downtown Durham.
 
Travel tip: the celebration will last until at least 6:00 pm, so don't get an early flight out on Saturday.

Hotels

Aloft Downtown Durham
We have a room block ($137/night) at this modern Aloft hotel, which is a 15-minute walk to Durham Central Park and at the center of many restaurants and activities downtown. This brand-new hotel is accepting reservations starting May 2015.
Durham Marriott City Center
Another downtown hotel option, less than 5 minutes' walk to Durham Central Park and many other downtown sites.

Taxis
