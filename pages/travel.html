Travel info

 
Travel tip: The celebration will last until at least 6:00 pm on Saturday, so we recommend flying out on Sunday.

Airports

RDU airport (Raleigh-Durham)
Nearest airport, about 20 minutes from downtown Durham.
CLT airport (Charlotte, NC)
The Charlotte airport is about 2 hours and 45 minutes from downtown Durham.

Hotels

Aloft Downtown Durham

A room block is available for you at $149/night through August 26. This modern Aloft hotel is a 15-minute walk to our Saturday celebration venue and is at the center of many restaurants and activities downtown. To book a room with our group rate, please contact the hotel's director of sales, Kay Hess, at 919-402-5656 and mention "Rhodes Family Event."

Or you can click this link: https://www.starwoodmeeting.com/Book/rhodesmcmackinfamilyevent

Durham Marriott City Center
Another downtown hotel option, a 5 minutes walk to the Saturday celebration venue and many other downtown sites.

Getting around town

Charlene's Safe Ride
Good moderate cab service for getting around town. Call 919-744-4444.
Bull City Connector
The Bull City Connector is a free, reliable bus route that goes through downtown. It can take you to many Durham hotspots like 9th Street, Duke University, and the Sarah P. Duke Gardens.
