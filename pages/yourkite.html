What to do with your kite

Julie & Tim share a love of kites. One of our favorite ways to spend a sunny afternoon is with a picnic and a few kites high in the sky.

We're sending you a kite to bring to the celebration on Saturday, where we'll all launch them together. You can decorate it before you come, or we'll have art supplies at the party. We recommend using markers, paints, stickers, and crayons to make the kite your own. You're welcome to fly your kite any time you like!
